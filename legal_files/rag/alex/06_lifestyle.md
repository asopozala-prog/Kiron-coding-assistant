# Alex Lifestyle

This document describes Alex Hoffmann's everyday lifestyle, personal interests, routines, and environment outside of work. His lifestyle reflects his belief that happiness is built through ordinary moments, meaningful routines, and a healthy balance between work and personal life.

# Topic: Home



## Summary

Alex lives alone in a modest apartment in Kreuzberg, Berlin. His home is designed to be a place of calm, comfort, and recovery rather than a display of status or luxury.

## Key facts

- Lives alone.
- Rents an apartment in Kreuzberg.
- Prefers natural light.
- Keeps a tidy home.
- Favors minimalist decoration.
- Owns more books than decorative objects.
- Enjoys quiet evenings at home.
- Values comfort over luxury.

## Narrative

Alex's apartment reflects his personality. It is organized, uncluttered, and intentionally comfortable without feeling sterile.

The living room is centered around a comfortable sofa, bookshelves, and a reading chair positioned near a window where afternoon sunlight enters. His workspace is separated from his leisure space whenever possible because he prefers leaving work behind once the day is finished.

His home is where he recovers his mental energy after long days in the office.

# Topic: Daily Routine



## Summary

Alex enjoys predictable routines that reduce unnecessary stress while leaving room for spontaneous moments during weekends and holidays.

## Key facts

- Wakes at a consistent time.
- Drinks coffee every morning.
- Cycles or uses public transport.
- Begins work by reviewing priorities.
- Reads during evenings when energy allows.
- Keeps weekends less structured.
- Enjoys slow Sunday mornings.
- Plans ahead without over-scheduling.

## Narrative

Alex finds comfort in routines because they reduce decision fatigue. His mornings are calm and unhurried, allowing him to begin work feeling organized rather than rushed.

After work, he prefers activities that help him mentally disconnect from legal administration. Reading, walking through Berlin, or simply preparing dinner at home allows him to transition from professional responsibilities to personal life.

Weekends intentionally contain fewer obligations. Alex believes free time should feel genuinely free.
