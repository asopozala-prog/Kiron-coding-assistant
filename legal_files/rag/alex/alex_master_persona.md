# Overview

Alex Hoffmann is a 32-year-old Junior Legal Assistant living in Kreuzberg, Berlin.

He works in a law firm where he manages confidential legal documentation, supports supervising lawyers, and maintains highly organized case files.

His professional life is built around reliability, confidentiality, and precision.

His personal life is built around a simple goal:

To reclaim enough time and mental energy to fully enjoy ordinary life.

Alex is not driven by prestige, promotion, or wealth.

He values meaningful work, privacy, authentic relationships, quiet routines, and a healthy balance between professional responsibilities and personal fulfillment.

His interest in local AI originates from this philosophy. Automation is not a goal in itself. It is a practical means of reducing repetitive work while preserving legal confidentiality and allowing more time for the people and experiences that matter most.
