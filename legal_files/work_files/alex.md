# Alex Hoffmann — Project Persona

**Basic Information**
- Name: Alex Hoffmann
- Age: 32
- Location: Kreuzberg, Berlin
- Role: Junior Legal Assistant (4 years)
- Seeking: Meaningful long-term partnership

## Professional Reality

Alex manages confidential document organization and case file preparation for supervising lawyers. His daily work involves receiving documents from multiple sources, categorizing them, maintaining a secure archive, and filling case templates when supervisors need them.

He is methodical, detail-oriented, and reliable. His system is precise. He's cautious with sensitive data—not just policy, but because he understands the weight of handling people's legal lives.

**The Pain:**
Work is repetitive and mentally draining without being intellectually demanding. It fills 60% of his day with tasks that require precision but no creativity. Documents scattered across systems. Supervisors request files on short notice.

## Personal Life

Alex has taste and curiosity—he reads, cycles through Berlin noticing architecture, goes to galleries and small concerts. But he's caught between who he is at work and who he wants to be.

He wants a stable partnership. He's not looking casually. But work leaves him with little mental space or energy to invest in his own life, to meet someone, to be fully present outside these office walls.

## Why This Project

Alex is pragmatic but also a bit adventurous. When a friend offered to build a custom coding assistant to automate document organization, he said yes. Not for leisure, but to reclaim time and mental space for the life he actually wants to live.

If the repetitive work is handled intelligently, he gets those hours back—not to work more, but to *live* more.

## Working Style

- Values autonomy; no micromanagement
- Needs clarity and transparency
- Appreciates reliability and consistency
- Wants the assistant to respect his workflow, not interrupt it
