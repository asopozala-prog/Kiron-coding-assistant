# Alex's Statement — The Life He Wants

---

You know what I miss most?

A slow Sunday morning. Sitting somewhere with good light, a decent coffee, a piece of cake, and just... reading. No emails in the back of my head. No case files I didn't finish on Friday.

And Saturday nights — I want to actually be present. Go out, meet someone, have a real conversation without my brain still half at the office. I'm gay, I want a partner, I want a love life. That doesn't happen when you arrive home depleted every single day.

It's not a big dream. Coffee, a book, someone to come home to.

That's what I'm working towards. Not a promotion. Not a bigger salary.

Just enough space to actually live.
