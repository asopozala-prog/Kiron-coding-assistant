# Alex's Statement to the Programmer

---

Look, I'll be honest with you.

I spend most of my day doing work that a machine could do better than me. Sorting emails, matching data, filling templates, cross-referencing documents. It's precise, it's repetitive, and it drains me completely by 6pm.

And I know the tools exist. I've seen what GPT, Claude, Copilot can do. I've tested them myself at home. They would cut my document processing time in half. Easily.

But I cannot use them at work. The firm is strict — no cloud-based AI, no external processing of client data. And honestly, they're right. One data leak, one confidential document processed on someone else's server, and we're facing a GDPR violation, a breach of attorney-client privilege, maybe worse.

So I sit there, knowing exactly what could help me, and I can't touch it.

By the time I leave the office I'm too tired to invest in anything — dating, social life, myself. The irony is that the solution exists. It's just not allowed to live in the cloud.

That's why I need this. Local. Private. Mine.

---
